WHAT IS GOENNOUNCE?

GoEnnounce is the new way for students to share their educational accomplishments and fundraise for goals. It’s a safe way for students, like you, to e-nnounce and track achievements, projects, and goals to get the encouragement and financial help you need to succeed! We provide you a place to tell your educational stories with the family, friends and mentors who can help you reach your goals. Create a GoEnnounce Student Page today and start inviting your network to follow your journey through school!
