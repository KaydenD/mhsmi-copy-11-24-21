BIRMINGHAM – In girls hoops action, Chloe Godbold scored 11 points as Farmington Hills Mercy edged Birmingham Marian 44-43 on Friday evening.

Jackie Bauer and Katie Coe scored 10 points. Jenna Schluter recorded seven points and Jess Mruzik scored six points had 10 rebounds for Mercy (2-1, 1-0 CHSL).

Olivia Moore scored 11 points. Lauren Montalbano, Hannah Redoute and Uche Ike all scored eight points for Marian (2-2, 0-1 CHSL).

More girls basketball

- Novi Franklin Road Christian 44, Auburn Hills Oakland Christian 27 - At Auburn Hills, Rachel Bause had 16 points and 11 rebounds. Daria Boboc had 12 points, nine rebounds and two blocks for Franklin Road Christian (4-0). Madison LaLone netted 10 points and Danielle Hopkins added six points for Oakland Christian.

- Holly 43, Linden 29 - At Holly, Paige Reid totaled 17 points and six rebounds, Becca Fugate added 16 points, four rebounds, five assists and five steals and Taylor Vines contributed four points and six rebounds for Holly, which outscored Linden 21-9 in the second half.

- Rochester Adams 50, Lake Orion 44 - At Rochester Hills, Maggie Rogers pumped in 21 points to lead the Highlanders. Hannah Sinard contributed seven points and 12 rebounds, while Makayla Smith chipped in seven points and nine rebounds for Adams. Sophie Wyborski netted 13 points, Maddie Novak put in eight points and Cameron Coltrin had six points for Lake Orion.

- Royal Oak 21, Fraser 8 - At Royal Oak, Samantha Potter had nine points and eight rebounds for Royal Oak (4-0). Fraser (2-1).

- Waterford Mott 39, Milford 29 - At Waterford, Emily Spencer had 12 points and Emily Huff added eight as Mott (3-0) kept its record unblemished. Mallory Barrett’s eight points paced Milford (0-2).

- Novi 46, Chelsea 34 – At Chelsea, Ellie Mackay scored 20 points, grabbed four rebounds, had an assist and two steals. Cara Ninkovich had six points, five rebounds and an assist. Mikayla Lynch added five points and an assist. Lauren Calhoun had six points and a rebound and Ally Pringle recorded four points and a rebound for Novi.

- Lakeland 32, West Bloomfield 31 - At White Lake, Lauren Castellon finished with 12 points, including putting in the game-winning bucket for Lakeland (2-2), while Skylar Baer supplied nine points and Kate Winter put in four points for the Eagles.

- Pontiac 54, Hamtramck 13 - At Hamtramck, Brala Benion had 17 points and Aniya Erkins supplied 10 for Pontiac (1-1).

- Stoney Creek 30, Birmingham Seaholm 12 - At Stoney Creek, Morgan Porter had 11 points and Emily Solek chipped in six for Stoney Creek (2-1). The game was called late in the third quarter due to an injured player.

- Dearborn 52, Pontiac Notre Dame Prep 39 – At Pontiac, Gretel Keller scored 10 points and Sarah Beckett scored eight points for ND Prep (0-3). Nasreen Kobeissi had 14 points for Dearborn (3-0).

- Bloomfield Hills 68, South Lyon East 36 - At Bloomfield Hills, Victorie Franklin notched 13 points for Bloomfield Hills (3-1), while Lucy Cronin tallied 13 points for South Lyon East (0-2).

- Clarkston Everest Collegiate 57, Austin Catholic Academy 15 - At Clarkston, Clare Nalepa totaled 14 points and 10 rebounds and Katelyn Allen added 11 points and 10 rebounds for Everest Collegiate (3-1).

Boys Basketball

- Clarkston 59, Orchard Lake St. Mary’s 45 - At Clarkston, Foster Loyer recorded 25 points. Dylan Alderson scored 20 points. C.J. Robinson added 23 points for OLSM.

- Royal Oak Shrine 56, Birmingham Roeper 23 - At Royal Oak, Ian Gappy totaled 11 points and Asher Smith added nine points for Shrine (1-1). Josiah Smith had 11 points for Roeper (0-1).

- Farmington 91, Detroit Old Redford Academy 66 - At Farmington Hills, Jordan Graham exploded for 32 points and Ray Bryant supplied 18 for the Falcons (1-1) in the North Farmington tournament. Tyrell Jackson-Davis contributed 14 points, Russell Norris tossed in 12 and Christian Smith added 10 for Farmington. Old Redford dropped to 1-1.

- Troy 75, Lake Orion 44 - At Troy, Jason Dietz finished with 26 points, Leon Ayers added 19 points and Danny Sully put in 10 points for Troy (1-0).

- Birmingham Groves 62, Redford Thurston 53 - At Beverly Hills, Drew Sheckell finished with 21 points and 13 rebounds, Marcus Pittman added 13 points and Charles Jones totaled 10 points and 10 rebounds for Groves (1-0). Demetrius Dowd had 15 points for Thurston (1-1).

- Waterford Our Lady of the Lakes 56, Flint Academy West 50 - At Waterford, Isaac Oliver finished with 13 points, Noah Robak added 12 points and Grant Ross had 12 points for Our Lady of the Lakes (1-1), while Brandon Boyd chipped in with 18 points for Flint Academy West (1-1).

- Birmingham Seaholm 70, Harper Woods 63 - At Birmingham, Ray Flynn poured in 29 points and Cooper Nixon added 15 for Seaholm (1-1). Graham Girdler added 11 points for the victors.

- Birmingham Brother Rice 55, Dearborn Divine Child 50 – At Birmingham, Mike Laurencelle had 15 points. James MacKenzie had 13 points. Jack Moran had 11 points for Brother Rice (2-0). Connor Blair had 25 points for Divine Child (1-1).

- Warren Michigan Collegiate 69, Troy Athens 67 (OT) - At Warren, Clark Joslin had 16 points. Taylor McCaskill had 14 points, five rebounds, four assists and three steals. Hunter Nelson had 12 points for Athens (1-1). Ty Lawrence scored 16 points and hit the buzzer-beater on a putback attempt of a missed three-pointer. Two other players recorded a double-double respectively with Jordan Davis 12 points and 10 rebounds and Antonio Simley 10 points and 10 rebounds for Collegiate (2-0).

- Royal Oak 62, East Detroit 40 - At Detroit, Tyler Dawson led the way with 24 points, while Justin Akahon contributed 16 in the Ravens’ opener. Royal Oak took control with a 25-3 run in the third quarter.

- Clio 42, Ortonville Brandon 37 - At Clio, Dylan Antcliff had 12 points for Clio on the Flint Metro League contest. Ethan Hunt led the Blackhawks with 20 points, seven steals and six assists. Nolan Tews added 12 points, 11 rebounds and five steals.

- Linden 59, Holly 55 - At Holly, Austin Harless notched 22 points, including hitting six three-pointer shots, and Luke Copley added 12 points for Holly (1-1). Linden is 1-0.

- Novi 74, Rochester 55 – At Novi, Traveon Maddox scored 27 points and had six rebounds for the Wildcats.
