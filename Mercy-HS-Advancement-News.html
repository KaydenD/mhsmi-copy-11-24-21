DONOR FUNDED CAMPUS UPDATES
Air Conditioning Added
Thanks to the generosity of donors from the 1970s Class Challenge and the 2021 Auction Principal’s Pick, seven classrooms in the South Hall received air conditioning, new lights, ceilings and a fresh coat of paint.
All Class Challenge and Principal’s Pick donors are invited to a classroom blessing on Saturday, September 25 at 3:00 p.m. during the 76th Reunion Celebration.
Golf Simulator in M-Zone
Mercy Golf Team students will be able to practice their swing in the newly added golf simulator! The room is located in the lower level M-Zone Athletic Training Center In Memory of Sister Regina Marie Doelker, RSM '56.
