HOMECOMING DANCE UPDATE

The following information was sent to all students in regard to the Mercy Homecoming Dance.

Mercy will have a Homecoming Dance this year for all grades on Sunday, October 3! The dance will take place outside under tents in the parking lot facing Middlebelt Road (Auditorium/Gym parking lot). Please pay close attention to the following details.

  • Students must arrive by 8:00pm.
  • Enter and exit the campus using the 11 Mile Road entrance and exit only.
  • Park in the 11 Mile Road parking lot (Flagpole lot) only.
  • Enter the dance using the Performing Arts entrance (down from the Flagpole entrance) only.
  • Students will not be allowed to return to their cars during the dance. Once students leave the dance, there will be no re-entry.
  • Dress appropriately for being outside and shoes must be worn at all times.

Online ticket sales will begin on Monday, September 20. Be on the lookout for an email from Mrs. Richter with details. If you have any questions or concerns regarding the Homecoming Dance please contact Mrs. Tarplin or Mrs. Richter.
FLU VACCINATION CLINIC
Mercy will host the annual free influenza vaccination clinic for Mercy students, staff and families in partnership with the Michigan Community Wellness Visiting Nurse Association (VNA) and Alana's Foundation. 
 
Tuesday, October 12, 2021
1:00pm-3:00pm
Mercy High School Lobby
Face Mask Required

A separate consent form must be completed (in its entirety) for each person who will be vaccinated. Parents must complete and sign the form for anyone under the age of 18.

Complete consent form(s) online here: https://flu.vna.org
Clinic ID: 766
Passcode: SPZU5G4

Mercy is happy to offer our facility for this clinic. On the day of the flu vaccination clinic, any person who has pre-registered will need to present a picture ID and the insurance card (or a copy of the insurance card) in order to receive the vaccination. Alana’s Foundation will cover the cost for those without any insurance.

﻿We hope you will take advantage of the opportunity for your daughter(s) and/or yourself. Please consult with your family physician regarding any questions about the vaccine. Please note: Only quadrivalent injectable flu vaccine is being offered. There will be no flu mist.

Service & Caring Committee
Attention all Mercy Families! As part of the Mothers’ Club Service and Caring Committee, we have created a new ministry here at Mercy that supports our very own community. This is a community site to help coordinate meals, rides, or other support to families in crisis (illness, injury etc) at Mercy High School. You can sign up on the Lotsa Helping Hands website here.
 
When a need arises, you will be notified by email. You will not be assigned until you volunteer to accept. Please pass this site info on to the other parents and invite them to sign up!
 
If you know of a family in need, please encourage them to reach out to Janet Cronin at janetmatt555@gmail.com. We will only use the private website to share information with those that volunteer. Please consider helping in this ministry.

Support the Mothers' Club
All mothers and female guardians of Mercy High School students automatically belong to the Mercy Mothers’ Club! Throughout the year, the Mercy Mothers’ Club organizes events for the Mercy community at large, our staff, our daughters, and our families. To help cover these expenses, we are requesting voluntary donations. We gratefully accept and appreciate all contributions at any level. Checks may be made to 'Mercy Mothers' Club' and dropped off during any meeting or to the Mercy HS Reception Area in an envelope labeled 'Mercy Mothers' Club Membership Donation'. THANK YOU!

NEW MERCY HIGH SCHOOL MICHIGAN BOOK
Images of America Mercy High School Michigan Book
By Patricia Montemurri / Arcadia Publishing

Over 16,000 women are graduates of Mercy High School. Since 1945, when it opened as Our Lady of Mercy High School in northwest Detroit, its graduates have embodied the school motto: "Women Who Make a Difference." This book commemorates Mercy High's 75th anniversary and reflects the impact of "Mercy Girls" on their communities, country, and around the world.

Purchase your copy for $20 from the Mercy HS Alumnae Office or order online for shipment here.
