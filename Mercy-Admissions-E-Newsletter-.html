Prospective students should submit an application by December 23, 2016 for the first round of acceptance letters which will be mailed Friday, January 27th, 2017. Take a selfie with your acceptance letter and send it to us at   jhearle@mhsmi.org. Your photo might be included in Mercy media!  
 
There is a rolling application deadline for those 8th grade students who plan to take the   High School Placement Test  on either February 4th or March 4th.

Contact the Admissions Office at admissions@mhsmi.org or 248-476-2484 with any questions. The application is provided by the Archdiocese of Detroit and powered by SchoolAdmin.

Spend a Day at Mercy!

Take a look at Mercy! 7th and 8th grade students are invited to spend one school day at Mercy to experience our environment. Take a tour and see the amazing campus, attend classes, meet students and teachers, enjoy a complimentary lunch, and experience life at Mercy firsthand.

To schedule a shadow visit day, please submit the online Shadow a Student Day Request form at least 3 days in advance.

Please note that space is limited each day and visits must be scheduled through a parent. Shadow Days will run through December 15 for first semester and January 9 - May 12 for second semester. There will be no shadows on Wednesdays.
