FOX 2 Detroit
  • Facebook
  • Twitter

  • Email

The college admissions scandal currently rocking prominent universities across the country continues to grow and has opened the door to many questions about the admissions process. 

To help sort through some of the confusing information, Fox2 invited Holly Bennetts, president of the Michigan Association for College Admissions Counseling, to the studio Sunday morning.
