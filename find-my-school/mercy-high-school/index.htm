Mercy High School

Welcome to Educational Outfitters' online uniform store! We look forward to serving you and providing your child with great-looking and long-lasting clothing.  Our goal is to make the uniform-buying process fun and enjoyable for you.  If you have any questions or need help please contact us at (248) 426-7273.

 

Although we strive to always provide the most up-to-date and accurate uniform options for your school, it is important to review your school's uniform policy to ensure you are purchasing an approved product and/or color for your child's gender and grade.  Please refer to the Dress Code prepared by your school for additional information.
