Dear Mercy Alumnae, 
 
As each season brings about change, this summer has brought forth a new opportunity for myself, the Mercy Community and more specifically the Mercy High School Alumnae. 
 
My name is Laura Merritt Everett '98 and I would like to introduce myself as your new Director of Alumnae Relations. In 2015, I joined Mercy as a staff member and have since worked in the Attendance Office. I was able to encourage students, work with our amazing staff and meet many Mercy parents. My favorite part of the position was building the relationships that would transcend beyond Mercy halls. 
 
While venturing into this new position, one thing still remains true. The Mercy Community needs you as well! As I walk the North and South halls of Mercy I am often overcome with nostalgia of my high school days. I would love for you to come walk the halls with me, share your memories and thoughts of how we can continue to build new relationships for the future. I am looking forward to meeting you! 
 
In Mercy,  
 
 
 
Laura Merritt Everett '98
Director of Alumnae Relations
