Dear Mercy Parents / Guardians & Students, 
 
We hope you are enjoying summer and are looking forward to the start of another wonderful year at Mercy! Please note the following important information listed below in regards to accessing student course schedules.  
 
The 2019-2020 student schedules will be posted on PowerSchool Friday, August 2 at 4pm. All students and families must be sure all required forms are submitted on Privit, student billing account is current, and there are no outstanding JUGs in order to access their course schedule.   
 
* Mercy uses Privit for required school forms. The Personal Details Form, Emergency Verification Form, Concussion Form, Permissions Form, and Medical Treatment Release Form  must all be signed and submitted online through your Privit account in order for a student to receive their course schedule. All incoming 9th graders, transfer students, athletes, and students taking a Physical Education class, will also need a physical performed on or after 4/15/2019, the Pre-Participation History Form, Communications Authorization Form, and the Assumption of Risk Form. 
  • Click here for Privit NEW Parent instructions to create an account, if you haven't already set up your new login.
  • Click here for Privit CURRENT RETURNING Parent Instructions to update your existing account. If you already have a Privit account for Athletics, you do not need to create another account. If you have a Privit account for an older daughter, you can add a younger daughter.  
  • Click here to login to Privit. Please allow approximately 15-20 minutes to complete all forms. Be sure to reference all instructions and directions and if you have any questions or issues, contact kmscalzi@mhsmi.org.
* Your daughter's schedule, Mercy email and book purchasing are not available if her Student Billing account (including iPad and tuition) is not current. If you have questions regarding her Student Billing account, please contact Mrs. Hayes in the Business Office at 248-893-3580 or arhayes@mhsmi.org. The Business Office is open 8:30am to 2:30pm. Please note the building will be closed Friday, July 26.

* Your daughter's schedule is not available if she has outstanding JUGs. If you have questions in regards to JUGs, please contact Mrs. Tarplin at edtarplin@mhsmi.org.

* If your daughter is an incoming 9th grader or transfer student, please be sure you have made note of iPad pick up dates the week of August 5th.  
 
Stay connected to your email and the Mercy web site for additional upcoming important back to school information like School Life Calendar, Uniforms, Fall Sports Information, Marlin Business Day, Book Purchasing, Opening Days of School, and more.
 
Thank you, 
Mercy Administration
