IU Previews

Your questions. Your backyard.

Which application do I choose? What else do I need to send? What are the application and scholarship deadlines? What is the average class size?

Every fall, IU Bloomington hosts IU Preview events across the country. At these presentations, experts from IU Admissions will tell you about our top-notch academic programs and amazing student experience. They’ll even walk you through the application and scholarship processes. It’s an informal event, you can bring guests, and it’s free.

We look forward to seeing you at a Preview in your area!

IU Preview locations and dates will be listed here when they are confirmed.
