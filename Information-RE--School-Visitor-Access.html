Dear Mercy Community,

Mercy High School continues to monitor the COVID-19 risks and plan preventative measures to help assure the health and safety of our students, faculty and staff.  
 
As a result, we find it necessary to limit visitor access to the building, academic areas, and campus. While we love to welcome our alumnae and community guests, we kindly ask you to refrain from visiting Mercy High School at this time. We are grateful for your partnership as we navigate together through this situation.
 
Please routinely check your email for health and safety communications as well as event updates and information from Mercy High School.
 
Learn more information about the status of Coronavirus from both the Center for Disease Control ( CDC ) and the Michigan Department of Health and Human Services ( MDHHS ). Your best source to answer frequently asked questions can be found at the CDC.
 
Thank you for your cooperation, support and prayers as we proactively prepare to protect our students and staff.
