Farmington Hills Mercy players pose with their MHSAA district championship trophy on Friday.

   Division 1 No. 1-ranked Farmington Hills Mercy rolled past Livonia Stevenson 3-0 to win the Michigan High School Athletic Association District 25 volleyball championship on Friday, Nov. 2, at Farmington.

   Mercy cruised 25-13, 25-10, and 25-16 to improve to 50-2-0 on the season.

    Mercy beat North Farmington 3-0 in the district semifinals.

   Mercy advances to Region 7 action at Bloomfield Hills Marian, where they will face Detroit Renaissance on Tuesday, Nov. 6. Catholic High School League rival No. 6 Marian takes on Detroit Cass Technical in the other regional semifinal. The regional final is Thursday, Nov. 8.
