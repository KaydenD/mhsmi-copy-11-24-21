2019 National Player of the Year: Mruzik Makes it Memorable

By Sloane Green
Posted on January 9, 2020

Mruzik (attacking) is the 2019 PrepVolleyball.com HS National Player of the Year

Its been a year to remember for Jess Mruzik. From the highest volleyball awards in the state of Michigan to the national level to across the Atlantic Ocean on the international stage, nearly all the awards a junior player can possibly accomplish have pointed to the PrepVolleyball.com National High School Player (Senior) of the Year, Mruzik.

The University of Michigan recruit capped off her high
school career with an MVP performance in Mercy HSs first state title
where she put down 34 kills with a .492 efficiency and had 15 digs.
