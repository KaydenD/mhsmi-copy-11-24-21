CollegeWeekLive by PlatformQ Education is no Longer Available

As of Oct 1, 2020, CollegeWeekLive by PlatformQ Education is no Longer Available.

Over the past 15 years, it has been an honor to support the millions of students, families and counselors who used our platform. As digital continues to evolve the college search and selection process, virtual college fairs have run their course.

PlatformQ Education now offers Conduit, an interactive platform that empowers you to run large-scale online events, live chats, and live streams across desktop, mobile, and social media. Through Conduit, you build stronger relationships with your audience via interaction on an owned and branded platform.
