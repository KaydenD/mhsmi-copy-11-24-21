WEDNESDAY, OCTOBER 13 REMINDERS
PSAT Testing Day for Sophomores & Juniors
Please reference communication here in regard to information for PSAT Testing Day tomorrow, Wednesday October 13 from 8:00am-12:00pm. All students also received an email of detailed instructions.
  • The morning of the exam, go directly to assigned testing room. Attendance/Sign-in will take place in your testing room. Testing starts PROMPTLY at 8:15 am. Testing Room assignment will be sent to everyone at least one day before the test.
  • Late students will NOT be permitted to take the test once the 1st section of the test has begun, as the tests are strictly timed.
  • Dismissal will be at approximately 12:00 pm for the day.
  • Remember to bring two #2 pencils & a calculator with you.
  • A list of approved calculators was sent to all students, so be sure yours is OK to use.
  • Please contact the Counseling Office with any questions.

Frosh Retreat Day
Please reference communication here in regard to 9th Grade Retreat Day tomorrow, Wednesday, October 13 from 8:00am-2:40pm. All students also received an email of detailed instructions.
  • Students should dress comfortably (uniforms not required)
  • Students must bring a lunch (Cafeteria will not be open, no food sales available)
  • The first portion of the day will be a spiritual retreat led by Campus Ministry and senior student leaders. Campus Ministry will provide donuts and apple cider as a snack. Students will be split up into small groups and move through different stations where they will learn about their gifts and talents and about the Sisters of Mercy and Catherine McAuley. Please contact Mrs. Becker with any questions at mkbecker@mhsmi.org.
  • The second portion of the day will be our 9th Grade Diversity Retreat, hosted by Mercy Link Crew. The goal of Diversity Retreat is to create a more connected and inclusive Class of 2025, through activities and discussion. Please contact Link Crew Coordinators Mrs. Brown (tmbrown@mhsmi.org) or Mrs. McGavin (samcgavin@mhsmi.org) with any questions.

SIGN-UP FOR PARENT TEACHER CONFERENCES
Conferences are scheduled to take place virtually via Zoom Thursday, October 21 from 1:00pm to 3:30pm and 5:30pm to 7:30pm. Five minute conferences with teachers will be scheduled using the online registration tool PTCFast.com. Counselors will schedule 15 minute meetings.

In order to schedule a parent-teacher conference, visit the following link and follow all registration directions:

https://www.ptcfast.com/schools/Mercy_High_School_1  

Please indicate a name on your Zoom account so that teachers can easily identify you in order to admit you. Please honor the five (5) minute time limit when meeting with each teacher.

Mercy Performing Arts Presents Radium Girls
Friday, October 29 at 7:30pm
Saturday, October 30 at 7:30pm

Tickets are $10 each and available beginning 10/6 online here.

Radium Girls by D. W. Gregory is based on the true story of a group of women literally and figuratively fighting for their lives in 1920s America. After continuous exposure to radium in the watch factory in which they work, several women become sick and eventually die. The play documents the court proceedings of Grace Fryer and her fight for justice against a corrupt corporation, while slowly succumbing to the poisoning.

NEW MERCY HIGH SCHOOL MICHIGAN BOOK
Images of America Mercy High School Michigan Book
By Patricia Montemurri / Arcadia Publishing

Over 16,000 women are graduates of Mercy High School. Since 1945, when it opened as Our Lady of Mercy High School in northwest Detroit, its graduates have embodied the school motto: "Women Who Make a Difference." This book commemorates Mercy High's 75th anniversary and reflects the impact of "Mercy Girls" on their communities, country, and around the world.
