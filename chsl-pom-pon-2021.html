Catholic High School League
2021 Pompon and Dance Championships

Thursday, March 11, 2021, at Riverview Gabriel Richard

Dance

 

2021 CHSL Dance Overall Division champions: Farmington Hills Mercy

Farmington Hills Mercy wins CHSL dance championship

Farmington Hills Mercy edged Ann Arbor Father Gabriel Richard to win the Catholic High School League Dance Championship. In past seasons, pompon and dance were part of a single championship in two divisions. Due to opt-outs due to the pandemic, the two specialties were separated into two championships in a single division.

Scores

1. Farmington Hills Mercy, 218.5

2. Ann Arbor Father Gabriel Richard, 216.0

3. Macomb Lutheran North, 194.5

4. Riverview Gabriel Richard, 184.0

Pompon

 

2021 CHSL Pompon Overall Division champions: Bloomfield Hills Marian

Bloomfield Hills Marian wins CHSL pompon championship

Bloomfield Hills Marian edged Dearborn Divine Child to win the Catholic High School League Pompon Championship. In past seasons, pompon and dance were part of a single championship in two divisions. Due to opt-outs due to the pandemic, the two specialties were separated into two championships in a single division.

Scores

1. Bloomfield Hills Marian, 134.5

2. Dearborn Divine Child, 131.5

3. Wixom St. Catherine of Siena, 110.5
