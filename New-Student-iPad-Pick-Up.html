Due to the ongoing COVID-19 health and safety precautions, please be aware of the following guidelines:

  • If you do not feel well or are experiencing any COVID-19 related symptoms, please stay home.
  • Masks are required to enter the building and must be worn at all times.
  • Students will enter at the flagpole courtyard entrance. Follow the signs to the Media Center. Staff will be on hand to guide students to the proper location.

Once everyone arrives for respective session, we will distribute the iPads to each student and walk them through the setup and basic use of their iPad. We expect this to take 30 to 60 minutes. We kindly request that you do not come to get your iPad at any other time or date than the one you have selected.

If you have any questions or need to schedule an alternative pickup day/time, please revisit the sign-up site or contact Chris Janusch at ccjanusch@mhsmi.org or Tom James at tfjames@mhsmi.org.

Thank you and we look forward to seeing you at Mercy!
