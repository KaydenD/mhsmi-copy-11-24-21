WELCOME TO MERCY!
VIRTUAL TRANSFER STUDENT ORIENTATION
Tuesday, August 18, 2020
1:00pm-2:30pm via ZOOM

We are so excited to meet and welcome our new Marlins! This year's new 10th and 11th grade Student Orientation will be hosted virtually via ZOOM on Tuesday, August 18 from 1:00pm to 2:30pm. 

All new transfer students will receive an invitation sent to their Mercy email address on August 17th which will include a registration link. Once registered, students will receive an automatic confirmation email with secure login information to be used at 1:00pm on the 18th. 

All new transfer students are required to attend and are asked to please login using their Mercy iPad if possible. This is a student-only event led by the amazing Mercy Link Crew. Please direct any questions or concerns to Mrs. Brown at tmbrown@mhsmi.org.
OPTIONAL SCHOOL BUILDING TOURS &
USED UNIFORM SALE
Wednesday, August 19, 2020
3:00pm-6:00pm 
Pre-Register for Tour Sign-Up Times

Mercy Link Crew will be offering small-group, in-person, zero contact tours of the school building for new students on Wednesday, August 19 from 3:00pm to 6:00pm. 

Tours are for students only (no parents/guardians) and pre-registration is required. We will not be able to accept walk-ins due to capacity limits of small groups.  

Students must wear a face mask upon entering the building and throughout the entire tour. Please direct any questions to Link Crew Coordinator Trish Brown at tmbrown@mhsmi.org. 

Please click here to sign-up for a tour time slot. 

The Mercy Mothers' Club will also be hosting a Used Uniform Sale outdoors in the Auditorium/Gymnasium covered entrance from 3:00pm to 6:00pm on Wednesday, August 19. 
  • Masks are required
  • The number of shoppers at one time will be limited to maintain social distancing 
  • Cash or check only
  • No fitting rooms available
Great deals on used uniform items: skirts, sweaters & quarter zips $20; long sleeve shirts $15; short sleeve shirts $10.
