MERCY MENTORSHIP PROGRAM
Be a Mercy Mentor! We are proud to announce the launch of the Mercy Mentorship Program designed to connect alumnae and students. We are now accepting applications for this new initiative which will begin in mid-March with our Senior Class of 2021.

Applications are due Monday, February 22 and selected Mentors will receive notification by March 1. If you are chosen as a Mentor, you will serve 2-3 young women within a virtual pod once a month. Mentors will provide direction, guidance, and facilitate pod sessions. The total time commitment is two hours per month for a commitment of one year, which includes one mentorship session and one training session. There will be a background check and you will need to complete the Protecting God's Children class.

Please view the application here and email alumnaeoffice@mhsmi.org with any questions.

You do not need to live locally to apply, all alumnae are welcome! We look forward to connecting you with Mercy students.
