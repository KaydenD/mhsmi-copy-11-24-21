Welcome to Mercy's Media Center

Mercy High School's Media Center is one of the most powerful research centers in the State of Michigan. Students, Faculty and Alumna can access our resources anytime/anywhere.  Alums can access a password by calling the Media Center at 248.893.3574.

The Media Center offers a variety of learning spaces and a wide variety of print and electronic resources.  Our print resources are located in the common area of the Media Center.


Mission of the Media Center:

  • Provide a safe and welcoming educational setting for students and faculty
  • Enhance student learning and achievement
  • Help students reach their academic potential
  • Support, enrich and complement the work of each classroom teacher
