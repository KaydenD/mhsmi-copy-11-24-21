Hometown Life 2018 Academic Top 6 - Oakland County

Hometown Life
View Comments

(Story updated May 8 to include college choices and other information received before deadline for some Sacred Heart students.)

Hometown Life congratulates the following students chosen as their school’s shining stars. Public and private high schools within the coverage area of Hometownlife.com/Observer & Eccentric Newspapers were invited to submit their Top 6 students to be honored for academic achievement. Each school was encouraged to submit the criteria for choosing its students and are presented in the order submitted, with many listed in alphabetical order.

In total 30 schools responded, 17 from Oakland County and 13 from Wayne County. In all, 176 students are represented. Of those that submitted test scores, the average SAT score is 1,494.93 and the average ACT score is 33.72. There were 17 students with an ACT score of 36 and one had an SAT score of 1,600.
