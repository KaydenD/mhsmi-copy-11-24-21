2019 JCAS Registration
The Joyce Ivy College Admissions Symposium (JCAS) is a series of workshops for high-achieving female high school students, families, counselors, and educators, led primarily by admissions and financial aid professionals from some of the nation's most rigorous and selective colleges. JCAS is an opportunity for participants to gather the information necessary to successfully navigate the college admissions and financial aid process at highly selective institutions, and for college access professionals to share best practices in a collaborative setting.
