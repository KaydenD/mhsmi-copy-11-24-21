WELCOME TO MERCY!
VIRTUAL FRESHWOMAN ORIENTATION
Wednesday, August 19, 2020
9:00am-10:30am via ZOOM

We are so excited to meet and welcome our new Marlins in the Class of 2024! This year's 9th grade Orientation will be hosted virtually via ZOOM on Wednesday, August 19 from 9:00am to 10:30am. 

All 9th grade students will receive an invitation sent to their Mercy email address on August 18th which will include a registration link. Once registered, students will receive an automatic confirmation email with secure login information to be used at 9:00am on the 19th. 

All 9th graders are required to attend and are asked to please login using their Mercy iPad if possible. This is a student-only event.

Please note that for security reasons, each student must register prior to 9:00am on August 19th.

USED UNIFORM SALE
Wednesday, August 19, 2020
3:00pm-6:00pm 
Pre-Register for Tour Sign-Up Times

Mercy Link Crew will be offering small-group, in-person, zero contact tours of the school building for incoming 9th graders and transfer students on Wednesday, August 19 from 3:00pm to 6:00pm. 

Tours are for students only (no parents/guardians) and pre-registration is required. We will not be able to accept walk-ins due to capacity limits of small groups.  

Students must wear a face mask upon entering the building and throughout the entire tour. Please direct any questions to Link Crew Coordinator Trish Brown at tmbrown@mhsmi.org. 

Please click here to sign-up for a tour time slot.

  • Masks are required
  • The number of shoppers at one time will be limited to maintain social distancing
