Increased Precautions We're Taking in Response to COVID-19

LAST UPDATED ON 08/20/2021

As updates on the impact of COVID-19 continue to be released, we want to take a moment to inform you of the heightened preventative measures we have put in place at Timberline Knolls Residential Treatment Center to keep our patients, their families, and our employees safe. All efforts are guided by and in adherence to the recommendations distributed by the CDC.

Please note that for the safety of our patients, their families, and our staff, there are certain restrictions in place regarding on-site visitation at Timberline Knolls Residential Treatment Center.

  • These restrictions have been implemented in compliance with updated corporate and state regulations to further reduce the risks associated with COVID-19.
  • Options for telehealth visitation are continuously evaluated so that our patients can remain connected to their loved ones.
  • Alternate methods of communication for other services may be offered when deemed clinically appropriate.

For specific information regarding these changes and limitations, please contact us directly.

CDC updates are consistently monitored to ensure that all guidance followed is based on the latest information released.

  • Temperature and symptom screening protocols are in place for all patients and staff.
  • All staff receives ongoing infection prevention and control training.
  • Thorough disinfection and hygiene guidance is provided.
  • Patient care supplies such as masks and hand sanitizer are monitored and utilized.
  • Physical distancing strategies have been implemented to ensure that patients and staff maintain proper distance from one another at all times.
  • Cleaning service contracts have been reviewed for additional support.
  • Personal protective equipment items are routinely checked to ensure proper and secure storage.
  • CDC informational posters are on display to provide important reminders on proper infection prevention procedures.
  • We are in communication with our local health department to receive important community-specific updates.

The safety of our patients, their families, and our employees is our top priority, and we will remain steadfast in our efforts to reduce any risk associated with COVID-19.

The CDC has provided a list of easy tips that can help prevent the spread of the coronavirus.

  • Avoid close contact with people who are sick.
  • Cover your cough or sneeze with a tissue and then immediately dispose of the tissue.
  • Avoid touching your eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Clean and disinfect objects and surfaces that are frequently touched.
  • Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • Stay home when you are sick, except to get medical care.

For detailed information on COVID-19, please visit the CDC website HERE.

Residential Treatment Center for Women & Girls

Individualized Care

Timberline Knolls provides individualized care for adult women and adolescent girls who have been struggling with eating disorders, substance use disorders, and mental health concerns. Each woman or girl who receives care with us completes a thorough assessment to identify her unique strengths, needs, and goals. This information informs the development of her individualized plan. Our commitment to individualized care means that no two women or girls will have identical experiences at Timberline Knolls. 

Compassionate Professionals

Care at Timberline Knolls is provided by a multidisciplinary team of experienced professionals. These compassionate experts work closely with each woman or girl to develop and deliver the focused services that will help them achieve true and lasting healing in mind, body, and spirit. When you receive care at Timberline Knolls, you will be encouraged to work in active collaboration with your treatment team, and you will be empowered to take ownership of your continued recovery.

Safe & Supportive Environment

At Timberline Knolls, we are committed to maintaining a safe, supportive, and welcoming environment for every woman or girl who is entrusted to our care. Our private campus is located on 43 wooded acres, with features that include carefully landscaped grounds and historical buildings. Our team works diligently to ensure that Timberline Knolls remains a safe space where all women and girls are treated with the dignity and respect they deserve. 

Worried or uncertain? Timberline Knolls can help.

We know this may be a difficult time for you and your family. There are many questions involved in understanding treatment for eating disorders, addiction, and the other co-occurring conditions we treat.

ANSWERS TO COMMON QUESTIONS FROM WOMEN, GIRLS, AND THEIR FAMILIES

What is residential treatment?

When you take part in residential care at Timberline Knolls, you can step away from the stresses and distractions of daily life so that you can focus your full attention on your health. During residential treatment, you’ll live in a comfortable room on our serene, 43-acre campus. Our residential programming features round-the-clock care and individualized services, all provided by compassionate professionals in a safe and welcoming environment.

How much can I expect insurance to contribute toward my care?

We work with all major insurance companies, and we welcome opportunities to partner with new organizations. Coverage for our services may vary from insurer to insurer. The best way to determine insurance coverage for your care at Timberline Knolls is to contact us. A member of our team will be happy to work directly with your insurance company to ensure that you have access to all benefits within your plan.

What distinguishes Timberline Knolls from other women-only rehabs or residential treatment centers?

Timberline Knolls provides individualized care, age-appropriate programming, and holistic services to promote lasting healing in mind, body, and spirit, all within a safe and welcoming environment that was designed specifically for women and girls.  

Throughout your time at Timberline Knolls, you’ll work with dedicated professionals and form mutually beneficial bonds with others who are receiving care. When you complete your time with us, you’ll receive a detailed discharge plan to guide your continued progress, and you’ll join our national alumnae network, which can be a source of ongoing support for years to come.

How do I learn more about types of eating disorders?

Learning more about eating disorders can be a vital step toward receiving the care you need. If you click on the “Eating Disorders We Treat” tab at the top of this page, you can access a wealth of information about several types of eating disorders. Our eating disorder programming includes a strong educational component to help you understand the causes and effects of eating disorders and develop the skills that will help you achieve improved health.

Where can I learn more about addiction?

Increasing your knowledge about substance use and addiction is an important part of the recovery process. This website can be a source of considerable information about the impact of addiction and the services that can help women and girls. For answers to specific questions about care for substance use disorders, please contact us directly at your convenience. A member of our team is available 24/7 to speak with you.

What will a woman or girl experience while receiving care at Timberline Knolls?

Every woman or girl who receives services at Timberline Knolls will have a unique experience that is based on her specific needs and goals. However, everyone who is entrusted to our care can expect to benefit from certain fundamental features and principles. For example, your care will include several types of therapy, you will work in active collaboration with a team of experienced professionals, and you will receive comprehensive support within a safe and welcoming environment. To learn more, click the “Programs” tab at the top of this page.

How does Timberline Knolls help women and girls who wish to see the principles of their Christian faith reflected in their care?

We are proud to offer faith-based care through The Grace Program. Features of this optional program include multiple Christian-focused groups, Christ-centered 12-Step recovery services, sessions with a Christian therapist, a dedicated sacred space on our campus for prayer and reflection, and opportunities to attend worship services within the community.
