        • About Us

          Helping Families of Prospective College Students Successfully Navigate the Complex Maze of College Admissions and Financial Aid

          Applying for college can be a stressful time for both students and parents. MCF provides the knowledge, expertise and resources to make sense of this journey as we assist you in finding a college that fits the student academically, socially and financially

          Comprehensive Solutions​

          Using a team approach to work with college-bound families, MCF provides a comprehensive service plan that helps clients overcome the challenges of college planning. Our experienced team guides students and their parents through the college selection and funding processes while utilizing our proprietary Student Positioning concepts

           

          Take the Stress out of the College Planning Experience​

          We are with you from start to finish, turning the process into progress, so that in the end, you're able to select a college with confidence, clarity and certainty

          With over two decades of collective experience, we have successfully helped students and their families arrive at the right choice through proven techniques, and programs developed specifically for this purpose
