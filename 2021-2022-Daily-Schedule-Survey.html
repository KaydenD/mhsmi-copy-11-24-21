Greetings Students and Parents/Guardians,

Mercy Administration is seeking your input as we consider the structure of the daily schedule for the 2021-22 school year. We look forward to our August return to a 5 day a week in-person schedule. We will also reinstate the cherished tradition of the 6 day rotating schedule with unscheduled hours. This format is unique to Mercy and prepares our students for college while allowing time for collaboration, dialogue with teachers and work or relaxation time as needed.

We have learned valuable lessons from our adjusted schedules during the 2020-21 school year and will consider retaining some of those best practices as we move forward. Your input is necessary so that we get this “right” for students and staff. Please complete a short survey to help us better assess what is needed to create a desirable schedule. Please note that your survey responses will help inform the schedule and no changes are made at this time.

Please complete the survey by Wednesday, July 21, 2021.
