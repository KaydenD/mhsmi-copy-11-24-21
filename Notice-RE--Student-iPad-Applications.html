Dear Classes of 2023 & 2024 Student & Parent/Guardian,

In April of 2020 when the COVID-19 pandemic forced all classes to a virtual format, it was decided to lift the restriction on student iPads to allow for the use of iMessage, FaceTime and Google Hangouts as a further means of student communication. 

As we return to all in-person classes this coming school year, please be advised that starting on Monday, August 23 these restrictions will be put back into place which will disable the use of iMessage, FaceTime and Google Hangouts. These application restrictions are meant to limit distractions during class time and when utilizing the iPad for school work at home.

Thank you for your cooperation and support. We look forward to seeing you back at Mercy!

Sincerely,

Mercy Administrative Team
