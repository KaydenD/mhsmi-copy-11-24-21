Shadow a Student Day Request

Mercy High School Farmington Hills, Michigan

8th Grade Students - Please complete the shadow request form below and indicate your first, second and third choice dates. We limit the number of shadows allowed each day and will do our best to accommodate your preferred date. We will email or call you with your official confirmation of your scheduled shadow date within three business days. If you aren't able to attend your confirmed day, please kindly notify the Admissions Office as soon as possible.

Please note we will welcome 8th grade students first semester and 7th and 8th grade students in the second semester.

Reference shadow day information and instructions here. If you have any questions, contact the Admissions Office at admissions@mhsmi.org or 248-476-2484.

Be sure to check your spam folder after submission for email confirmation and mark Mercy High School (mhs@mhsmi.org) as a safe sender. We look forward to welcoming you at Mercy!
