Evrod Cassimy

Reporter/Anchor

Evrod Cassimy is the morning anchor for Local 4 News Today. He joined WDIV in August of 2013.

He is an award winning journalist and a six-time Emmy Award nominee. In 2010 he won the Virginia Associated Press Award for "Best Breaking News Coverage."

Evrod was born in Michigan but grew up in the Chicagoland area. He, graduated from Columbia College Chicago with a degree in Television Broadcast Journalism. He began his TV news career in Grand Junction, Colorado and has covered news in Madison, WI, Orlando, FL, Richmond, VA, and Denver, CO before joining the Local 4 News team.

When he’s not on TV or writing stories for Local 4, Evrod enjoys writing music. He is an accomplished recording artist. His music is available on all digital outlets (iTunes, Spotify, Apple Music etc) and has been heard on radio across Metro Detroit. In 2018, he started his own record label, Everything Evrod Entertainment, and released his debut album.

Evrod and his wife have two children.
