Local College Info Events

Published on 09/20/2016

Dear Sophomore, Junior and Senior Parents:
 
Please find listed below some upcoming local college information events. We hope you are able to take advantage of these college admission sessions.
 
Thank you,
The Mercy High School Counseling Office
An Evening with the University of Notre Dame
You and your family are invited to join us for an ND on the Road information session presented by Mary Katheryn Snyder, a member of our Admissions staff. The session will include information on academic, community, and spiritual life at the University of Notre Dame, as well as insights into the admissions and financial aid processes. Alumni and parents of current students will also be in attendance to share what Notre Dame means to them.
 
The event will be held on 
Thursday, September 29th at 7 pm in the Mercy High School Auditorium. Guests need to enter the parking lot off of 11 Mile Road and should enter the building at the Auditorium/Lobby Entrance which directly faces Middlebelt Road. 
Please RSVP your attendance for the session by completing the form below. We look forward to seeing you!

Knights of Columbus Catholic College & University Fair
 
Brighton's St. Patrick Knights of Columbus, the Archdiocese of Detroit, the Dioceses of Lansing and Saginaw invite students to participate in the 2nd annual Catholic College and University Fair, planned for October 5, 2016 from 4:30-8:00 pm at Cleary University in Howell, MI.  The main goal of this college fair is to provide local high school students and their parents the opportunity to speak with representatives from multiple Catholic colleges to gain a better understanding of:
  • Navigating the complexities of financial aid and scholarships   Presenter:   Eric Wagner V.P. of Finance Ancilla College
  • New process for filling out  FASA applications  Presenter:  Sally Foster Senior Financial Aid Officer
  • Maintaining a Catholic Faith while participating in College Sports.   
In addition, a keynote speaker will share their experience as a catholic athlete in college and beyond. All students from both public and private high schools in Livingston, Oakland, Washtenaw, Genesee and Ingham counties are invited to attend. There is no cost for attending and free pizza and beverages will be served for all in attendance.
If you have any questions, please visit us at  http://www.catholiccollegenight.org/  or call (810) 225- 9973.
