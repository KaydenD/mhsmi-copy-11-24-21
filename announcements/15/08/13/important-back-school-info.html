Important Back-to-School INFO!

Published on 08/13/2015

IMPORTANT NOTIFICATION: All required school forms must be turned in Monday, August 17 for all students. Students will not be allowed to attend classes if all forms are not turned in by 8:20am, Tuesday, August 18.
 
Welcome to an exciting new school year! There are many events planned and we want every student and every family to be well informed. Check below for a brief outline.

New this year - Click on the Event on the 2015-16 Mercy School Life Calendar for event details. The "Description" box will give more information and "Attachment" will give the time schedule.

Mercy School Life Calendar

Opening Days Events, Times, Locations

Friday August 14th All 9th Grade Students
Please arrive at the flagpole entrance no later than 9:15am. Bring fully charged ipad and wear casual clothing. (Please note: Casual clothing includes jeans, capris, longer shorts. Spaghetti strapped or strapless tops, short shorts, pajama pants or ripped clothing are NOT to be worn to school at any time.)

  • Turn in Emergency Verification Form and Health Forms
  • Tech orientation 9:15 am - 12:15 pm (dismissal)
  • Marlin Shop open 12:15 pm - 1:15 pm (sells Mercy apparel)
Friday, August 14th All Transfer Students
Please arrive at flagpole entrance no later than 9:15am. Bring fully charged ipad and wear casual clothing. (Please note: Casual clothing includes jeans, capris, longer shorts. Spaghetti strapped or strapless tops, short shorts, pajama pants or ripped clothing are NOT to be worn to school at any time.)       
  • Tech orientation 9:15am-12:15pm
  • Lunch is provided
  • Transfer orientation 12:15pm - 2:30pm (dismissal)
  • Marlin Shop open 12:15pm - 1:15pm (sells Mercy apparel)
Link Leader Commissioners involved with transfer orientation report to Drama Studio by 12:00pm.   
Monday, August 17th FRESHWOMEN WELCOME DAY All 9th Grade Students
Please arrive at the Gymnasium / Auditorium entrance by 8:00am. Lunch is provided. Wear casual clothing. School photos (Picture Day ID LM625218Q0) and ID photos taken. Dismissal at 2:30pm.
 
All Link Leaders report to gym by 7:00am. Wear Link Crew shirt.
 

Monday, August 17th Handling Your Business Day All 10th-12th Grade Students

Please drop-in at the Gymnasium / Auditorium entrance from 9:00am-11:00am or 1:00pm - 4:00pm. School photos and ID photos taken. Parking passes, Yearbook orders (order number 13826) and required school paperwork collected.  

Handling Your Business Day Forms 

Tuesday, August 18th First Full Day of School All Classes 

All grades sign-in with adviser 8:00am - 8:10 am. No unscheduled class periods today. Please bring a lunch from home. Link Leaders wear Link Crew shirt and it's a Dress Nice Day so no jeans or sweats; longer, dressy shorts are permitted.
 
Uniforms will be worn starting Wednesday, August 19!
NEW PARENT ORIENTATION
 
Tuesday, August 18
6:00pm - 7:30pm
Mercy High School Auditorium

New Mercy parents are invited to learn about Mercy culture, technology at Mercy, policies on behavior and attendance, how you can support your daughter and more!
