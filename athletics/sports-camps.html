Summer 2021 Sports Camps at Mercy

The Mercy Athletics Office is excited to announce sport clinics and camps for girls of all ages held on the Mercy Campus. COVID-19 health and safety protocols in effect.

BASKETBALL

Join Mercy Basketball Alumnae and Coaching Staff, including 18th year Mercy Varsity Basketball Coach, Gary Morris. During his time at Mercy, Coach Morris has led the Marlins to 7 Central Division, 5 Catholic High School League, 12 District, and two Regional Championships; along with one Final Four Appearance. He has been selected Coach of the Year by the Detroit Free Press, the Oakland Press, the Daily Tribune, Catholic League Coaches Association and the Basketball Coaches Association of Michigan.

Our focus is to provide an opportunity for each young lady to improve her basketball skills and learn new basketball concepts while making friends and having an enjoyable week in a relaxed atmosphere. The camp is designed to provide an educational, competitive environment while allowing players to have fun while improving their basketball skills through group and individual participation.

  • Individual, group instruction, and drills designed to improve fundamental basketball skills and help each player gain confidence in her own abilities

  • Staff-to-player ratio of 1-to-6

  • Skill and competitive contests with various prizes and awards

  • Camp T-Shirt for each player

VOLLEYBALL

Join us this summer for a series of volleyball camps hosted by the Mercy High School volleyball staff. All  players will be instructed by Varsity coach Loretta Vogel. Loretta Vogel brings over 45 years of volleyball  coaching experience. In 2020, Loretta Vogel won National Coach of the Year; Michigan Womens’ Volleyball Coaches Hall of Fame inductee, 2012, became the 6th coach in Michigan to reach 1,000+ career wins, including a State Championship!

  • All  levels and abilities are welcome. 

  • No volleyball experience is necessary. 

  • Camps are not exclusive to Mercy High School students. 

  • All campers will receive a camp t-shirt.
