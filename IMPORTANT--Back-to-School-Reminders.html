STUDENT SCHEDULES
The 2021-22 student schedules are posted on PowerSchool. As a reminder, all families must be sure all required forms are submitted on FinalForms and your student(s) billing account (including iPad and tuition) is current in order to access Mercy student email, course schedules and book purchasing. 

  • If you have any FinalForms questions, please email kmscalzi@mhsmi.org.
  • If you have questions regarding your daughter's Student Billing account, please login to your Smart Tuition account or the Business Office at 248-893-3580 or mmueller@mhsmi.org.
  • If you are unable to access PowerSchool using your username and password, try resetting your password using the "Having Trouble Signing In?" link. Once in PowerSchool, the "Grades & Attendance" icon will give the list of classes for the whole school year. The "Exp" column lists the hour the class meets. To see a matrix view for the first semester with room numbers click the "My Schedule" icon. Any periods not listed are unscheduled periods.  
 
Please note: The master schedule was built (sections and staffing) based on student course selections made in the Spring. Alternates, at times, were used to complete schedules when necessary. Students with unresolved conflicts in their schedules were emailed. Please have your daughter check for an email regarding a schedule conflict (current students) or from the Admissions Office (new students) before emailing her counselor. The only other schedule changes counselors will handle, the first week of school, are straight drops (except AP or Honors courses per contract signed to enroll) or adds (if space is available). Schedules will not be rearranged to accommodate a "change of mind."

BOOK PURCHASING
The BNC K-12 powered by MBS Virtual Bookstore will go live on Friday, August 13th, 2021 and free shipping runs until August 26, 2021. BNC K-12 powered by MBS has worked hard with our Mercy Staff to provide a comprehensive list of books needed for your daughter's courses.

Shop by Schedule enables students to enter their Mercy Student ID to access their entire schedule's course materials on the Virtual Bookstore. This allows your daughter to add all of her course materials to her shopping cart at one time, eliminating the need to select each course individually. *Please note the shop by schedule feature is only available for students who completed required school forms and billing account as of August 10.

Shop by Course is also available using your student schedule. Feel free to experience this easy and convenient ordering method by visiting BNC K-12 powered by MBS Virtual Bookstore.

  • Click the "Let's Get Started" button on Mercy's Virtual Bookstore.
  • Under "Shop by Schedule", enter your student ID number.*
  • Confirm that you are that Mercy Student - click "Continue."
  • You'll see your course materials and can select items to check out when ready!

Take advantage of digital formats and free shipping offers! Since every Mercy girl will have an iPad to facilitate her learning, consider digital formats in lieu of a hardbound textbook. Read and follow the Teacher Course Messages listed and carefully check your order details before checkout. 

Please note that some Math and Science online course books will be acquired once school begins using an access code provided directly from teachers. English teachers may provide a list of books that students will purchase and download throughout the school year. 

Contact our MBS books liaison Kelly Bickes at kabickes@mhsmi.org with book ordering questions.
