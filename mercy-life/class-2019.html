CURRENT ANNOUNCEMENTS

2019 Graduation Ceremony on Vimeo

This year, the graduation ceremony is being shared via Vimeo which will allow you and your family to view the video on any device and download it to make a copy on any format you would like for your own personal use. To view and obtain your copy, please click here for spreadsheet of personally assigned links and codes for each family. Each family has a unique code so please only use your assigned code. Once you have accessed the sheet, please follow the following steps:

  • Copy and paste your personal link into your browser. This link can be found in column D. It will take you to vimeo.com and automatically apply the Promo Code.
  • Enter your corresponding promo code. Be careful. These codes are case sensitive.
  • If you do not have FREE Vimeo Account, please follow the on-screen prompts to create your own personal account. You will be prompted to enter your first and last name and a username and password. Please use your email address as a username and create a unique password.
  • This account is your personal account and has no affiliation to Mercy High School or the filming company. There is no need to enter any credit card information as it has been prepaid.
  • If you already have an account, simply log-in and you will be prompted: "Congrats! Your code is verified."
  • Once you are into vimeo.com, please simply follow the on-screen prompts to either Watch Now or Download. You can also find it under Purchase and Watch Later on any screen or device of your choosing.
Please enjoy, and if you have any questions, feel free to contact the senior moderators at seniormoderator@mhsmi.org.
 
Graduation Photos
 
Everyone will be photographed at Mercy graduation. Photos will be online and available for ordering from Lifetouch at events.lifetouch.com.
 
Mercy Google Account
 
Please be aware that on August 1, 2019 your Mercy Google account will be deleted. This means all email and any files you have uploaded to your Mercy Google drive will be deleted. If you wish to transfer any of your content here is a link to Google Takeout which will allow you to download a copy of most of your content and upload it to a personal Google account. Also if you have created any accounts on other sites or cloud based services (Dropbox, Box, Apple etc.) using your Mercy email address you will have to change those accounts to use a personal email address instead before August 1st, 2019. Congrats on your graduation and as always if you have any further questions stop by the Tech Office.

GRADUATION TICKETS
  • Each student will receive 8 graduation tickets and may pick up her tickets beginning this Wednesday, May 8. The graduate does not require a ticket to attend the ceremony.

  • Each graduate will be allowed to request up to 12 extra graduation tickets for a total of 20 tickets. The extra ticket request form requiring a parent signature can be found here and submitted to the Reception Area. Students will be notified when their extra tickets are available.
  • The last day to request extra graduation tickets is Wednesday, May 22. There will be no ticket processing during Mercy Day of Celebration on Thursday, May 23.
  • Senior families will be notified beginning Tuesday, May 28 of any available tickets beyond the 20 allotment.

GRADUATION TICKETS
 
Each graduate will receive eight (8) admission tickets for the graduation ceremony by picking them up and signing them out at the reception area. Graduates do not need a ticket. Extra graduation ticket request form found here. Tickets will not be given to any senior who has not fulfilled all of her school obligations of detentions, financials, MCAP requirements, or who has not completed her senior survey.
 
Fulfillment of obligations include:
  • Detentions. Contact or see Mrs. Tarplin in the Dean's Office.
  • Financial (tuition, sweepstakes) Contact Business Office or Dr. Kreger.
  • MCAP requirement (service hours completed and reflection paper submitted) Contact Mrs. Griggs-Dennis in the Pastoral Ministry Office.
  • Senior Survey Contact Mrs. Burek in the Counseling Office.

June 2 - SENIOR ALL NIGHT PARTY

Following graduation, all seniors who have submitted required permission forms will enjoy one last night with each other as a class. Mercy Lobby doors will open for check-in at 10:15pm. Students will board buses and leave Mercy at 10:45pm for Joe Dumars Fieldhouse. The ending time is 6:00am. Students are asked to wear shorts and closed-toe shoes. They do not need to bring anything and phones will be left at Mercy so the students can truly enjoy being together one last time. If you have any SANP questions, please contact the senior all-night party committee or Associate Principal Colleen McMaster at cmcmaster@mhsmi.org.
