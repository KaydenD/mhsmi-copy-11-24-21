Class of 2022

CLASS OF 2022

Check often for announcements, links, updates. All seniors are asked to frequently login and check the Class of 2022 Schoology Page for news from your Senior Class Officers and Moderators.

 

ANNOUNCEMENTS

Senior Event at Paradise Park

A lock-in evening of fun and games for the Seniors only at Paradise Park in Novi on Saturday, November 6 from 10:30pm-1:30am. Cost is $30 per student and includes go karts, trampolines, arcade games, laser tag and more! Reserve a spot by submitting permission slip found here and payment to the Reception Area by October 25. The Senior Moderators are looking forward to an awesome event.

Yearbook Senior Portrait

Seniors must have their yearbook photo taken at Prestige Portraits to be pictured in the yearbook and the class composite. Locations can be found at their website prestigeportraits.com. Visit the studio’s online booking calendar for each location: Novi; Royal Oak; Oakland Township; Shelby Township. Due to yearbook deadlines and requirements, please keep in mind that you must have your picture taken at a Prestige Portraits studio by October 23, 2021 to appear in the yearbook and class composite. Poses must be picked out by November 6, 2021. If you have any questions, please feel free to call Prestige Portraits. 

Photos for Memory DVD

All photos for the class memory DVD will be submitted by Senior students to the Class of 2022 Schoology page under Media files. Deadline for photos: 

  • Baby Picture - December 14
  • Freshwoman Year - September 30
  • Sophomore Year - October 29
  • Junior Year - November 30
  • Senior Year - April 12

Parent/Guardian REMIND Text Notifications

If you would like to sign up for Class of 2022 Parent/Guardian REMIND app text notifications, please click here and follow directions.

Parent Congratulatory Yearbook Ads

Offer congratulations and recognize your daughter's efforts by placing a student ad in the Yearbook! These full color ads can include both text and photo(s). This is a wonderful way to pay tribute to your senior daugther! You may design these ads yourself or through the yearbook publisher website yearbookordercenter.com and use code 13826 or click here for order form. Ads due December 1, 2021.

Graduation Gown Approval By May 1

In order to participate in the graduation ceremony, every student must have her white graduation gown approved by Dr. Kreger or her assistant, Mrs. Wojcik. Graduation gown requirement letter found here.

IMPORTANT DATES

October 1: Senior Tailgate 7:00am-7:45am

  • Watch for a Google Doc from the Class Officers for what to bring and further instructions.

October 15: Senior Blessing Mass "We're All in this Together"

  • Attendance is mandatory. No early senior release.
  • During 6th-7th Hour (1:15pm)
  • Wear cap, gown and medallion
  • Livestream

October 23: Senior Photo Yearbook Deadline for Prestige

December 1: Yearbook Congratulatory Ads Due

November: Senior Quotes Due

March: Service Hours and Reflection Paper

April: End of Year Promise Due

April 30: Senior Survey and Scholarship Notification Deadline

May 1: Graduation Gown Approval Deadline

May 12: Senior Prom at the Roostertail

  • 7:00pm-12:00am. Must arrive by 7:30pm and cannot leave before 11:00pm

  • Roostertail Dress Code

  • Table Seating

  • End of Year Promise must be submitted in order to purchase Prom tickets

May 16-17: Senior Final Exams

May 18: Mercy Celebration Day

May: Baccalaureate Mass

  • Final celebration Mass. Wear cap and gown.

  • Flower presentation to the Blessed Mother by Seniors after Mass

  • Senior Farewell presentation and speeches

  • Emily George Award and Heart of Mercy Award

June: Mandatory Graduation Practice

  • If you are not present, you are not allowed to walk at graduation. You must bring the shoes you are wearing for graduation also so you can practice walking the stage in them. Do not make appointments/work/etc on the same day. We may run over and will not allow anyone to leave until we are done.

  • All graduation dresses must be approved before rehearsal.

June 6: Graduation Ceremony Date

  • A professional photographer will take pictures of each graduate as she receives her diploma

  • Video is taken of the entire ceremony and will be posted online to Vimeo. The Vimeo link and login information will be sent when available so you can view, download and save the 2022 graduation.
