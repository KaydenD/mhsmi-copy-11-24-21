What IS Catholic College Night?

Catholic College Night is an annual  college fair where students, and their parents, can personally meet with recruiters from Catholic colleges all around the area. You can have questions answered, hear about all the great programs they offer, learn what campus life is like, and catch one of our talks covering new information you need to know to make the right choice.

If we haven’t hooked you yet- we also have *FREE* pizza.
