Tuition Assistance

Through the generosity of donors to the Stewards for Tomorrow Campaign, an endowment fund was created which provides tuition assistance grants to our Catholic families. Each year, awards will be made to offset the K-12 tuition expenses for children of Catholic families who both reside within the Archdiocese of Detroit AND whose children attend Catholic schools located within the boundaries of the Archdiocese of Detroit.

The FACTS application, Parent Information Sheet and Pastor’s Signature form are available below. The Parent Information Document details the process, deadline dates and other essential information. It is important to read this document in its entirety before you apply for tuition assistance.

If you have any further questions regarding tuition assistance please contact your school’s tuition assistance coordinator.

Dear Parents,

Due to the extension of the 2020 income tax filing deadline, our 2021-22 Round 1 Tuition Assistance application deadline will be extended to Friday, May 21, 2021. The Round 2 application deadline will remain as June 30, 2021.

    Scholarships Available

    • Del Papa-Grimaldi-Gabriele

      College scholarship for current high school seniors or college freshmen residing in the Archdiocese of Detroit.

      Del Papa-Grimaldi-Gabriele Endowment Fund Scholarship
      Qualifying StudentsCurrent high school seniors or college freshmen residing in the Archdiocese of Detroit and are planning to attend college or university full-time in the 2021-2022 academic year.
      Participating SchoolsApplicant may attend an accredited college or university in the United States that has been approved by the fund committee.
      GradesCurrent high school seniors and first-year college students
      Family EligibilityChosen by the fund committee
      Term of AssistanceOne year
      Maximum AmountUp to $2,000 paid directly to the school
      Application DateApplication deadline is June 25, 2021.

    • Lilo Wendell Scholarship

      The Lilo Wendell Scholarship is a college scholarship for Catholic women.

      Qualifying StudentsHigh school seniors and first-year college students who are Catholic daughters of single mothers and who are members of a Parish in the Archdiocese of Detroit and are planning to attend college or university full time in the 2021-2022 academic year.
      Participating SchoolsApplicant may attend an accredited college or university in the United States that has been approved by the fund committee.
      GradesGraduating high school seniors and first-year college students
      Family EligibilityChosen by the fund committee
      Term of AssistanceOne year
      Maximum AmountUp to $4,000 per student paid directly to the school.
      Application DateApplication deadline is June 25, 2021.
      Application & More InformationDownload Application Form with link included.
