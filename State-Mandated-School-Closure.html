Dear Mercy Families,

We are aware of Governor Whitmer's mandated closing through April 6 for all K-12 schools within the state of Michigan.  Mercy High School will enact an online model for continuation of student learning which will be shared with students and parents. Our student support strategy will evolve and further develop as we process and evaluate the impact of this extended school closure. 

Students are encouraged to continue to check Schoology and stay connected to their email for course updates. We will begin mandatory online student classes on Wednesday, March 18. 

We understand that this is a challenging time for all of our Mercy families. You are in our thoughts and prayers. Please know more information is forthcoming and we are committed to maintaining a high quality Mercy education for our young women.
