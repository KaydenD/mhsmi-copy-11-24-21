HIGH SCHOOL PLACEMENT TEST PREP COURSE
The HSPT Prep Course program will focus entirely on preparing eighth grade girls for the November 20th and the December 4th placement test in the Archdiocese of Detroit. The program includes one 3-hour session. Students can earn better grades, score higher on the High School Placement Test, and earn scholarships. They will be more motivated and confident because they have the tools to succeed!

HSPT PREP COURSE AT MERCY HS
Saturday, October 2, 2021
Morning session: 8:00am - 11:00am
Afternoon session: 12:00pm - 3:00pm

The course will be facilitated by Mrs. Zena Samona-Ibrahim ’96. Mrs. Ibrahim taught middle school science at Our Lady of Sorrows school, and has been instructing a prep course for the HSPT for over 17 years. We are thrilled to welcome her back to Mercy to prepare our prospective students for this exam. The course will focus on helping students acquire greater expertise in reading, verbal, math, and test taking skills. The subjects covered will include: 
 
●       Reading comprehension and vocabulary
●       Grammar, usage, and writing skills
●       Algebraic operations
●       Problem solving
●       Time management
●       Test taking skills  
 
The course will cost $100 and will include a copy of "The Peterson’s Master the Catholic High School Entrance Exam." Snacks and water will be provided.
