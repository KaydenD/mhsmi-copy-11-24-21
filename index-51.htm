Welcome to NISCA- National Interscholastic Swim Coaches Association 
 
NISCA is a professional organization that supports High School coaches of all aquatic sports. They are dedicated to coaches education and athlete recognition through the All America programs. NISCA allows opportunities for coaches to network and exchange knowledge and experience

Journal Mailing 5/2020

Empty
  •  Print 
  • Email
Details
20 April 2020

Attention! 

The next NISCA Journal will be mailed to the HOME address listed in your NISCA membership. Please make sure that your address is up to date and correct. You can update your NISCA membership information by clicking on MEMBERSHIP and scrolling down to Editing Membership Information and clicking on that link. The direct link is https://www.be.niscaonline.org/membership/login.aspx?ReturnUrl=/membership/members/editprofilenew.aspx

If you have not reset your NISCA password on the website, you will need to do that first. Click on "Forgot Your Password?" on the right side of the page, enter your email address and follow the instructions in the email that you receive. Remember that your User Name is your NISCA number. When setting your new password, please do not use the # & * characters. 

If you do not receive the email, first check your spam folder. If it is not there, contact the webmaster with your name, school, NISCA number and a non-school email address to use for your NISCA membership account.

After discussion with the NISCA Executive board, we do not wish to risk the health of any of our members.  Therefore, the 2020 NISCA Annual Conference will be cancelled.
