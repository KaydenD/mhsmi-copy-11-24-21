Tuesday, August 22 Opening Mass and First Full Day of School All Classes 
All grades report to Adviser room by 8:00am. Opening Mass and Assembly 8:30am-9:50am. All parents are welcome! No unscheduled class periods today. Please bring a lunch from home . Link Leaders wear Link Crew shirt and students should adhere to *Special Dress Day guidelines listed below.

Uniforms will be worn starting Monday, August 28.  
  • While out of uniform August 22-25, students should adhere to the *SPECIAL DRESS DAY requirements: Special dress days for holidays, liturgies, or other special events should be viewed as dress-up days rather than dress-down days. Guidelines for special dress days direct that attire be appropriate for school. It is inappropriate to expose cleavage, mid-section, or hips. Ripped clothing, jeans, pajama pants, sweatpants, casual shorts, or spaghetti straps are not allowed. Dress shorts may be worn as long as they are fingertip length or longer. Leggings or yoga pants may not be worn as stand-alone pants, only as an accessory to a dress or skirt.
  • Please be aware of the following uniform policies. Students will receive a detention for any violation to these or any other uniform policy. (Student Handbook p. 7-8)
Skirt: The kilt should be worn no more than two inches above the knee. Any student who permanently shortens her uniform skirt to an unacceptable length may be required to purchase a new skirt.       
Legwear: Students must wear solid, without a pattern, black, dark green, gray, maroon, navy or white (no neon or lace) foot/leg wear with their uniforms. The colors may not be combined. Footless tights or leggings may be worn under the uniform skirt, but socks must be worn as well.

Homecoming Date Change
Please be advised of an important date change on the 2017-18 Mercy school life calendar. The Homecoming Dance scheduled for Sunday, September 24 will be moved to Sunday, October 1. This change was made to avoid conflicting with Catholic Central's Boys' Bowl activities and Homecoming Dance also scheduled for  Sunday, September 24. We apologize for any inconvenience, however, we would like to avoid the potential conflicts that may arise for our girls by having our dance and Catholic Central's on the same day. 

Along with moving the dance, Spirit Week will now be  Monday, September 25 through Sunday, October 1. There will be no school on  Monday, October 2. School will now be in session on  Monday, September 25.

All changes are reflected on the 2017-18 Mercy school life calendar which can be found online here.
