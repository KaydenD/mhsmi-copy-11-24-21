Last update: 8/3/2021

Please note: For the safety of all involved, please read and follow the following protocols:

  • All Athletes must have completed FinalForms profile.
  • All Athletes must have submitted the Registration Form.
  • If any of the following symptoms present, or there are Yes answers to any question on the form, PLEASE STAY HOME. It is the responsibility of the parent/guardian to enforce this. Please help us to keep the Mercy community safe.
  • Fever of over 100.3⁰F
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Headache
  • New loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
  • Congestion or runny nose
  • Nausea, vomiting, and/or diarrhea

  • If symptoms present, STAY HOME. Returning to activities requires that the Athlete be completely symptom-free for three days (72 consecutive hours), and bring a doctor’s note with diagnosis.
  • Coaches are also required to screen themselves for symptoms and stay home if unwell.
