Congratulations!
2021 Distinguished Alumna & Trailblazer Honorees
The Mercy High School Alumnae Association is pleased to announce the recipient of the 2021 Distinguished Alumna Award, Dr. Patricia Petrella Nouhan '81. The Distinguished Alumna honors a women who makes a difference in her walk of life and exemplifies and supports Mercy's values of Human Dignity, Mercy, Justice, Service, and Option for the Poor.

We are also proud to present the inaugural year recipient of the Trailblazer Award to Lauren Babb '10. This award is for amazing alumnae who have graduated within the year 2000-2016 and are to be commended for their incredible efforts to make a difference early in their career. 

Mercy President Dr. Cheryl Delaney Kreger '66 will present Dr. Nouhan and Lauren with their awards during our Special Edition: Virtual Tea for Tuition on Sunday, April 25, 2021 at 2:00pm via livestream here. We invite you to join us! Learn more here.
