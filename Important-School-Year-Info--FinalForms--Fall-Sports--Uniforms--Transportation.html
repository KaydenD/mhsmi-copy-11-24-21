PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS & FALL SPORTS INFO
Physical Requirements
All incoming 9th graders, transfer students, athletes, and students taking a Physical Education class, will need a current physical, and complete the FinalForms Profile. 

For the 2021-22 school year, you will need a physical performed on/after 4/15/2021.
Please upload to FinalForms or scan and email to athletics@mhsmi.org.

Fall 2021 Athlete Info
All athletes are required to have completed the FinalForms Profile. Registration for Fall 2021 is required to try-out and students should complete survey here by August 3. Please select all sports in which you are interested in FinalForms, as well as completing registration survey.

Try-out times will be on the Athletic Calendar and team specific pages as available. Please be prepared to attend all try-out sessions. Fall sports (Cross Country, Equestrian, Field Hockey, Golf, Swim & Dive, Volleyball) start date is tentatively August 9, 2021. Please stay tuned for specific sport team updates the week of August 2.

UNIFORMS
Each class wears the same color kilt for two years. Freshmen and sophomores wear plaid patterns, juniors and seniors wear solid colors. Mercy uniform attire is available for purchase exclusively from Educational Outfitters. All grades wear banded white or maroon polo tops (short or long sleeve), banded white blouses (short or long sleeve), and black sweaters (cardigan, v-neck or vest).

Everyone is encouraged to go to Educational Outfitters for fitting and purchasing. Only black sweaters purchased from Educational Outfitters, black quarter-zips purchased from the Marlin Shop and Mercy Varsity letter sweaters may be worn in addition to the required uniform blouse or polo and kilt (not in place of the blouse or polo). 

  • Class of 2022 - grey kilt
  • Class of 2023 - black kilt
  • Class of 2024 - blue plaid kilt
  • Class of 2025 - green plaid kilt
