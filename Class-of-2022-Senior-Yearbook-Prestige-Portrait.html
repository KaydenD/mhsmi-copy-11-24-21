Class of 2022 Senior Yearbook Portrait Information
Dear Mercy Parents/Guardians & Seniors of the Class of 2022,
 
The senior portrait – is there a more captivating or cherished remembrance of the senior year? We expect the very best portrait program for our seniors. That is why Prestige Portraits was selected as the official photographer for Mercy High School. Following are just a few of the benefits Prestige Portraits offers:

  • Unsurpassed service and value. Prestige Portraits offers early-bird specials and other incentives to fit any budget. Assistance is also provided to help select the best package to fit your needs.
  • Full-service yearbook support. Your yearbook photos are delivered to Mercy for you.
  • State-of-the-art facilities. We encourage you to visit their convenient locations to check out the newest sets and backgrounds. Plus, each studio has steamers, hair dryers, and health and beauty supplies to make sure you look your best.
  • Class composite. As a gift to you and the school, Prestige Portraits provides a class composite for the school and a personal copy for every student photographed. You must be photographed by Prestige Portraits to appear in the yearbook and the class composite.
 
Prestige wants to make your experience as enjoyable as possible, so please call the nearest studio to book your sitting. Locations can be found at their website prestigeportraits.com. ﻿Schedule your appointment early, as they cannot guarantee availability of appointment times.

For quicker scheduling, visit the studio’s online booking calendar for each location:

  • Novi
  • Royal Oak
  • Oakland Township
  • Shelby Township
 
Seniors must have their yearbook photo taken at Prestige Portraits to be pictured in the yearbook and the class composite. Neither bare shoulders nor exposed cleavage is permitted. Those who do not comply with the dress code or do not adhere to the deadlines will not be included in the yearbook and class composite.
 
Due to yearbook deadlines and requirements, please keep in mind that you must have your picture taken at a Prestige Portraits studio by October 23, 2021 to appear in the yearbook and class composite. Poses must be picked out by November 6, 2021.
 
If you have any questions, please feel free to call Prestige Portraits. Their professional staff is looking forward to hearing from you. Have a great summer!
